# HTTPS

> Estimated Reading Time: 8–10 minutes
> Difficulty: Beginner
> Prerequisites: HTTP

## Introduction

HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure) is the encrypted version of HTTP. It protects communication between clients and web servers using TLS.

---

## What You'll Learn

- Why HTTPS exists
- TLS encryption
- Certificates
- Secure web communication

---

## Why It Matters

Without HTTPS, attackers on the same network could read or modify unencrypted web traffic.

## Real-World Example

When you log in to your bank, HTTPS encrypts your username, password and other sensitive information.

---

## Cybersecurity Context

Security analysts verify TLS certificates, investigate HTTPS traffic, and identify insecure websites.
