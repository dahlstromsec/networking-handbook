# Email Protocols

> Estimated Reading Time: 8–10 minutes
> Difficulty: Beginner
> Prerequisites: TCP

## Introduction

Email relies on multiple protocols. SMTP sends email, while POP3 and IMAP retrieve it.

---

## What You'll Learn

- SMTP
- POP3
- IMAP
- Differences between retrieval protocols

---

## Why It Matters

Understanding email protocols helps troubleshoot delivery problems and investigate phishing campaigns.

## Protocol Overview

| Protocol | Purpose |
|---|---|
| SMTP | Send email |
| POP3 | Download email |
| IMAP | Synchronize email |

---

## Cybersecurity Context

Email remains one of the most common attack vectors through phishing, malicious attachments and spoofing.

---

## Summary

SMTP sends email while POP3 and IMAP allow users to receive and manage messages.
