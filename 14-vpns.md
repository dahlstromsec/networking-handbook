# Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)

## What You'll Learn
- VPN purpose
- Encryption
- Remote Access VPN
- Site-to-Site VPN

## Benefits

- Secure remote access
- Protect data in transit
- Connect remote offices

## Cybersecurity Context

VPNs encrypt traffic over untrusted networks and are widely used for secure remote administration.

## Summary

VPNs provide confidentiality and integrity for network traffic across public networks.
