# VPNs

> Estimated Reading Time: 8–10 minutes
> Difficulty: Beginner
> Prerequisites: HTTPS

## Introduction

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates an encrypted tunnel between devices across an untrusted network such as the Internet.

---

## What You'll Learn

- What a VPN is
- Remote Access vs Site-to-Site VPNs
- Why VPNs use encryption
- Common business use cases

---

## Why It Matters

Organizations use VPNs to securely connect remote employees and branch offices.

## Real-World Example

An employee working from home connects to the company VPN before accessing internal servers.

---

## Cybersecurity Context

VPNs protect data in transit but should be combined with strong authentication, such as Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

## Summary

VPNs provide encrypted communication across public networks and enable secure remote connectivity.
